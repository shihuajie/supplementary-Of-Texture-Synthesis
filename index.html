Texture synthesis

Analysis and Controlled Synthesis of Inhomogeneous Textures

This supplementary material basically contains three parts. The first part is the additional results of our algorithm in progression control, orientation control and combined two controls, which are respectively the supplementary of Figure 5, 8 and 10 of our paper. The 2nd part contains three comparison results: (i) comparison with Xue et al. [Xue2008] in progression map generation, (ii) comparison with Lukac et al.[Lukac2015] in orientation control, and (iii) comparison with discrete label maps of [Rosenberger09] and [Lockerman16]. They are respectively the supplementary of Figure 6, 7 and 9 of our paper. The 3rd part shows the results of time varying weathering texture synthesis, which is an application of our method. The corresponding sequences of guidance maps are also included there.
